Breakpoint Calculation:

Breakpoint Price The share price where Options and RSUs have equal net value. Above this price, Options are better. Below this price, RSUs are better. = (Strike Price The price you pay to exercise each option √ó Number of Options Total options you can buy with your budget ) √∑ (Number of Options Total options you can buy - Number of RSUs Total RSUs you can buy with your budget )
What this means:

‚Ä¢ If you think the stock will stay below this price ‚Üí Choose RSUs
